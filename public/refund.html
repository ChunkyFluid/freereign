Refund Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Try before you buy. FreeReign offers 10 free tools so you can fully evaluate the product before purchasing Pro. Because of this, all Pro purchases are final.

1. Digital Product — Immediate Access

FreeReign Pro is a digital product that grants instant access to all 25 tools and additional features upon purchase. By completing your purchase, you acknowledge that:

  • You are purchasing a digital product with immediate access
  • You have had the opportunity to evaluate FreeReign using the 10 free tools before purchasing
  • You consent to immediate delivery and acknowledge this waives any applicable withdrawal period (per EU Directive 2011/83/EU, Article 16(m))

2. When We Will Issue a Refund

We will issue a full refund if:

  • The product does not work as described — Pro features fail to unlock, tools do not function, or a critical technical issue prevents you from using the product
  • You were charged in error — duplicate charges or unauthorized transactions

Refund requests for technical issues must be made within 7 days of purchase and include a description of the problem. We may ask for a screenshot or brief explanation so we can fix the issue for everyone.

3. When We Will Not Issue a Refund

Because FreeReign provides a generous free tier for evaluation, we do not offer refunds for:

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Not needing the product anymore
  • Finding an alternative product
  • Failure to use the product

4. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify for a refund, email us at support@freereign.dev with:

  • Your order number or the email used for purchase
  • A description of the technical issue

We'll respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.

5. After Refund

Once a refund is processed:

  • Your Pro license will be deactivated
  • You'll retain access to the 10 free tools
  • Your payment will be returned to your original payment method

6. Contact

For refund requests or questions, contact us at support@freereign.dev.
