Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

The Short Version

FreeReign runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Period.

1. Data Collection

FreeReign does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies or browser fingerprints
  • IP addresses
  • Any data you input into the tools

2. Client-Side Processing

All CSS generation, previews, and tool functionality runs 100% in your browser. No data is sent to our servers. Your tool configurations and preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage and never transmitted.

3. Third-Party Services

FreeReign uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — to load Inter and JetBrains Mono typefaces. Google may collect anonymized usage data per their privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare — for DNS and CDN services. Cloudflare collects anonymized, aggregate analytics (page views, countries) without cookies or personal data.
  • Lemon Squeezy — for Pro license purchases only. Payment processing is handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy. We do not see or store your payment details.

4. Pro License Data

When you purchase a Pro license, Lemon Squeezy (our payment processor) handles the transaction. They collect standard payment information necessary to process your purchase. FreeReign does not have access to your credit card details.

5. localStorage

FreeReign uses your browser's localStorage to save:

  • Theme preference (dark/light)
  • Pro license activation status
  • Basic anonymous usage counters (never transmitted)

This data stays on your device and is never sent anywhere.

6. Children's Privacy

FreeReign does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, this is not applicable.

7. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Since FreeReign does not collect or process personal data, GDPR and CCPA obligations related to data processing do not apply. You have nothing to request deletion of because we never stored anything.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if we add features that involve data processing. Changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at support@freereign.dev.
